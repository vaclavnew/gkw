Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to play?

GrabKeepWin is designed for safe play when used as directed. The magnetic holders release cleanly without sharp edges. However, this is a contact game involving physical movement. We recommend:

  • Playing on soft surfaces when possible
  • Warming up before intense play
  • Avoiding grabs near the neck or face
  • Stopping immediately if anyone feels pain
  • Supervising players under 16
What are the materials and dimensions?

Holder: ABS plastic with embedded N52 neodymium magnets. Dimensions: 8cm x 5cm x 2cm. Weight: 45g each.

Strap: Adjustable nylon webbing with quick-release buckle. Fits waist 60-120cm.

Where do you ship?

We ship to EU countries, USA, UK, Canada, and Australia. Free shipping on all orders. Delivery typically takes 5-10 business days after shipping.

Can children play?

We recommend ages 12+ for unsupervised play. Children 8-12 can play with adult supervision. The game involves physical contact and quick movements, so players should be mature enough to play safely and follow rules.

How do I clean and maintain the equipment?

Wipe the holders with a damp cloth. The straps can be hand-washed with mild soap and air-dried. Do not machine wash. Keep away from extreme heat and direct sunlight for extended periods.
